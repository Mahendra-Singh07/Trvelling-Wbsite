Valley of Flowers(3N/4D)

Spiritual Himalyan Journey | Garhwal Region

Location

  • Region: Garhwal, Uttarakhand
  • Shrines: Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, Badrinath
  • Starting Point: Delhi
  • Ending Point: Rishikesh

🗓 Duration

  • Total Days: 12
  • Nights Stay: 11
  • Best Season: May to October
  • Difficulty Level: Moderate
  • Group Size: 6–15 people

🗓 Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1–Delhi to Govindghat

  • Early morning departure
  • Long drive via Haridwar, Rishikesh, Srinagar
  • Reach Govindghat by evening
  • Overnight stay

Day 2–Trek to Ghangaria

  • Drive to Pulna (trek start point)
  • 10 km trek to Ghangaria
  • Check-in at camp/hotel
  • Rest & acclimatization

Day 3–Valley of Flowers Trek

  • 4 km trek to Valley gate
  • Explore alpine meadows, wildflowers, glacier streams
  • Photography & packed lunch
  • Return to Ghangaria by evening
  • Overnight stay

Day 4–Hemkund Sahib Trek

  • Steep 6 km trek to Hemkund Lake & Gurudwara
  • Spiritual experience at 4,300m altitude
  • Return to Ghangaria
  • Overnight stay

Day 5–Return to Govindghat

  • Trek back to Pulna
  • Drive to Govindghat / Joshimath
  • Rest & local sightseeing

Day 6–Return to Delhi

  • Early morning departure
  • Scenic drive via Rishikesh
  • Tour ends with nature-filled memories

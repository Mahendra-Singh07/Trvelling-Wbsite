Spiritual Himalyan Journey | Garhwal Region

Location

  • Region: Garhwal, Uttarakhand
  • Temple: Kedarnath Jyotirlinga
  • Altitude: ~3,583 meters
  • Base Point: Sonprayag / Gaurikund
  • Nearest Towns: Guptkashi, Rudraprayag

🗓 Duration

  • Total Days: 4
  • Nights Stay: 3
  • Best Season: May to October
  • Difficulty Level: Moderate
  • Group Size: 6–10 people

🗓 Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1

  • Departure from Delhi to Guptkashi
  • Scenic drive via Haridwar & Devprayag
  • Check-in at hotel, dinner & rest

Day 2

  • Early morning drive to Sonprayag
  • Trek to Kedarnath (18 km) via Gaurikund
  • Temple darshan, overnight stay near temple

Day 3

  • Return trek to Sonprayag
  • Drive back to Guptkashi
  • Local sightseeing (if time permits)

Day 4

  • Return to Delhi via Rishikesh
  • Optional Ganga Aarti stop
  • Tour ends

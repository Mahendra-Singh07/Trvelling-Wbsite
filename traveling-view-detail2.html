Chardham-yatra(11N/12D)

Spiritual Himalyan Journey | Garhwal Region

Location

  • Region: Garhwal, Uttarakhand
  • Shrines: Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, Badrinath
  • Starting Point: Delhi
  • Ending Point: Rishikesh

🗓 Duration

  • Total Days: 12
  • Nights Stay: 11
  • Best Season: May to October
  • Difficulty Level: Moderate
  • Group Size: 6–15 people

🗓 Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1–Delhi to Haridwar

  • Morning departure from Delhi by AC vehicle
  • Scenic drive via Meerut, Muzaffarnagar
  • Evening Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri
  • Overnight stay in Haridwar

Day 2–Haridwar to Barkot

  • Drive through Dehradun & Mussoorie
  • Stop at Kempty Falls (optional)
  • Reach Barkot by evening
  • Dinner & rest at hotel

Day 3–Yamunotri Temple Trek

  • Early morning drive to Janki Chatti
  • 6 km trek to Yamunotri Temple
  • Darshan & holy dip in Yamuna Kund
  • Return trek & drive back to Barkot
  • Overnight stay

Day 4–Barkot to Uttarkashi

  • Drive along Bhagirathi River
  • Visit Vishwanath Temple in Uttarkashi
  • Check-in at hotel, rest & dinner

Day 5–Gangotri Temple Visit

  • Early morning drive to Gangotri
  • Darshan at origin of River Ganga
  • Visit Bhagirath Shila & Gauri Kund
  • Return to Uttarkashi by evening

Day 6–Uttarkashi to Guptkashi

  • Long scenic drive via Tehri Dam
  • Lunch en route, photo stops
  • Reach Guptkashi by night
  • Overnight stay

Day 7–Kedarnath Trek

  • Drive to Sonprayag, then Gaurikund
  • 18 km trek to Kedarnath Temple
  • Darshan & overnight stay near temple (camp/dharamshala)

Day 8–Kedarnath to Guptkashi

  • Return trek to Gaurikund
  • Drive back to Guptkashi
  • Rest & recovery day

Day 9–Guptkashi to Badrinath

  • Drive via Chopta & Joshimath
  • Visit Narsingh Temple en route
  • Evening darshan at Badrinath Temple
  • Overnight stay

Day 10–Badrinath to Joshimath

  • Morning visit to Mana Village (last Indian village)
  • Drive to Joshimath
  • Local sightseeing & rest

Day 11–Joshimath to Rishikesh

  • Drive via Rudraprayag & Devprayag
  • Evening visit to Ram Jhula & Laxman Jhula
  • Overnight stay in Rishikesh

Day 12–Rishikesh to Delhi

  • Breakfast & checkout
  • Return journey to Delhi
  • Tour ends with blessings & memories
